# Life in the UK Test - Research Findings

## The Official Test Format (2026)
- **Questions**: 24 multiple-choice questions
- **Time Limit**: 45 minutes
- **Pass Mark**: 75% (18 out of 24 correct answers)
- **Cost**: £50 per attempt at official test centres
- **Based on**: "Life in the UK: A Guide for New Residents" 3rd Edition
- **Pass rate**: ~64% nationally (about 1 in 3 people fail)
- **Retake**: Must wait 7 days between attempts, pay £50 each time
- **Pass doesn't expire** - can be reused for ILR and later citizenship

## 5 Chapters Covered
1. **The Values and Principles of the UK** - Democracy, rule of law, individual liberty, tolerance
2. **What is the UK?** - England, Scotland, Wales, Northern Ireland, Crown dependencies
3. **A Long and Illustrious History** - From Stone Age to modern times, key events, figures
4. **A Modern, Thriving Society** - Population, religion, customs, sport, arts, leisure
5. **The UK Government, the Law and Your Role** - Parliament, elections, voting, the law, your role
